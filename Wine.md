Here is a discussion of wine.
"Varietal" wines are made from a single grape variety.
What varietal interests you?

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cabernet_Sauvignon Cabernet Sauvignon]; Cabernet Sauvignon is one of the world's most widely recognized red wine grape varieties.
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chardonnay Chardonnay]; Chardonnay is a green skinned grape variety used to make white wine.
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Merlot Merlot]; Merlot is a red wine grape that is used as both a blending grape and for varietal wines.
Other varieties ;;

There are many more varieties of wine resulting from blending. 
Note that these are not varietals unless they are made from a single type of grape.
