One good band, if you like bluegrass, is Old Crow Medicine Show. Check out their song, Wagon Wheel http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1gX1EP6mG-E

Next, listen to Ryan Adams’ Oh My Sweet Carolina http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eMZYRvDvgT4
